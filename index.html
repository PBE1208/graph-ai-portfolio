About Me

Hello! This portfolio showcases my assignments for the "Generative AI for Work & Research" course. After being impressed by the R coding basics offered by the Graph Network, I have taken up this new course which will help me further leverage Gen-AI for ever-more productivity and innovation.

I have an extensive background in global public health, specializing in pharmaceutical regulation, data management, and the development of information-sharing systems and networks. I have operated at both policy and technical levels, always in international and multi-cultural environments.

I currently work for the World Health Organization (WHO), where I manage the Global Surveillance and Monitoring System on substandard-falsified medical products. In parallel, I am also doing a PhD with the Institute of Global Health at the University of Geneva.
